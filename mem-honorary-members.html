Honorary members

Home Honorary members

Starting from the estimation of the contribution of outstanding individuals - structural engineers from abroad, to accomplishment of MASE praogramme and Statues targets, instigation technical - technological information and, in general, to past development of Macedonian Association of Structural Engineers, General Assembly of MASE assigns the title Honorary Member of MASE.

The title Honorary Member of MASE is assigned to foreign individuals only

The Honorary Members of MASE are:
