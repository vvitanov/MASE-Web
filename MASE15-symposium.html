MASE 15

Main Topic:

50 YEARS SKOPJE EARTHQUAKE,
50 YEARS ASEISMIC DESIGN

Some events remain permanently etched in human memory. Such is the case with the Skopje earthquake of 1963. Event that caused a shift in construction engineering not only in Macedonia and former Yugoslavia, but much wider. MASE marks the 50th anniversary of the earthquake devoting his 15th international symposium to quality design and construction, retrofit, structural repair, monitoring and maintance of structures as the basic prerequisite for protection against natural disasters.

In cooperation with the Chamber of certified architects and certified engineers of Republic of Macedonia, round-table conference: “Certificates - rights and responsibilities“ will be organized within the framework of the Symposium.
