Sponsorship

Call for sponsorship

The symposium will enable a specifically dedicated exhibition area for product and technology demonstrations of companies relevant to the field. If you have any questions, please contact us by e-mail: mase@gf.ukim.edu.mk or nakov@gf.ukim.edu.mk, as well as by mobile +38978292917.

MASE remains committed to providing a forum for experience exchange, collaboration, knowledge progressing and fostering research, all vital to our community. However, all of this is not possible without the support from the relevant institutions and companies. Please select sponsorship package that suits your institution/company the best. Sponsorship packages and the account details are shown on the next page.
